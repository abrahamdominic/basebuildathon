**default:** Platform-specific default (typically slide_from_right on iOS, slide_from_bottom on Android).

**fade:** Fades in the new screen.

**fade_from_bottom:** Fades in and slides the screen up from the bottom.

**flip:** 3D flip animation.

**none:** No animation, instant transition.

**slide_from_right:** Slides the screen from the right.

**slide_from_left:** Slides the screen from the left.

**slide_from_bottom:** Slides the screen from the bottom.

**simple_push:** Generic push animation.

**These options allow for a variety of animations depending on the desired transition effect between your screens.**
